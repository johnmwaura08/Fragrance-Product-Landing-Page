La Fragrance

Vigeure` Eau de Parfum cannot be ignored. The mix of delicate aromas and strong wood notes creates a fragrance that is rather unique. The main bergamot fragrance dazzles as it is married to a host of sweet and fresh essences like orange blossom, petitgrain, and grapefruit as well as the flamboyance of musk and amber. For those who are so inclined, the manufacturer markets this scent as vegan and cruelty-free.
